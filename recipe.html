Tamagoyaki(Fried egg)

This is simple and traditional egg meal in Japan.Also it's a one of famous home cooking.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of water
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • 2 pinches of sugar
  • oil

Procedure

  1. Put mayonnaise in a bowl and add water.
  2. Add eggs,sugar and salt then mix up.
  3. Pour 2 in a heated pan and when it starts harden then roll it up.
  4. Repeat 3.
